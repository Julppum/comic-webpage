Cycle of Gaia

Fantasy comic in development.

About the story

Alex has spent his life watching his family suffer a tragic fate. The desire for revenge against his father’s murderer has lingered in his thoughts since childhood, yet the perfect moment has never come—until now. When military convoys crash in his quiet village, and a foreign prisoner named Alexandra seizes the opportunity to escape, Alex realizes his chance has arrived. He sets off to escort Alexandra to the capital city of Onsilo, intending to deliver her at the gates and slip into the city unseen—to finally kill General Ulaf, the man who took his father’s life. But his path is far from simple. Alexandra is no mere helpless captive—she has a strong will and unwavering faith in her goddess’s guidance. Along the way, they cross paths with Nenoviar, a mysterious mage who claims their journey is destined, their fates foretold in ancient prophecies. Alex refuses to be part of any grand plan. His only desire is vengeance, and nothing more. Yet, as the trio faces relentless obstacles, it becomes clear that unseen forces are shaping their destinies. Will Alex break free from the path laid before him, or is he merely a pawn in a far greater game? As their journey unfolds, they uncover truths about themselves, each other, and the world around them. But above all, they challenge the higher powers—proving that fate is never truly set in stone.
